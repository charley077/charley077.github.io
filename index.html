About Me

I am a master's student at the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS), and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS). I began my research journey in 2022 under the guidance of Prof. Xiaoming Deng. Prior to my master's studies, I earned my B.S. from Beijing Normal University (BNU).

My research focuses on 3D vision, where I aim to bridge the gap between reality and imagination by advancing methods for 3D reconstruction and content generation. I am particularly interested in reconstructing real-world scenes and objects while exploring innovative approaches to generate interactive 3D content from text or images. My ultimate goal is to create a virtual world that rivals, and even surpasses the real world.
